FAQ

Honest answers to the things everyone actually asks.

Is food provided?+
No — this is accommodation only. There are several food vendors, restaurants and a canteen within 5 minutes of the building. We made this choice deliberately to keep the rooms clean and the rent honest.
Is the deposit refundable?+
Yes. ₹7,500 is the security deposit, returned in full within 7 days of move-out via bank transfer. We deduct only for documented actual damage — no arbitrary "wear and tear" charges, no painting fee.
What is the minimum stay?+
We prefer monthly or longer. Daily stays (₹400/night) are available for short work trips. For monthly pricing, call or WhatsApp — rates vary by room type and are not listed publicly.
Do you take both working professionals and students?+
Yes. Our residents are a mix of working professionals, consultants, government employees and students. The community is quiet and respectful — that's the standard we hold regardless of profession.
Is parking available?+
Yes — covered parking for bikes and cars, included in the rent. No separate parking charge.
Are there hidden charges?+
No. The only additional charges are the ₹250/month surcharge for personal electric appliances (iron box, heater etc.) and the ₹7,500 refundable deposit at move-in. Rent is rent — nothing else is added afterwards.
